# About Fluid Trading Systems

Fluid Trading Systems is an educational project focused on understanding how markets behave through structure, volatility, and risk.

This repository exists to document ideas, frameworks, and concepts used to analyze price behavior — not to provide signals or financial advice.

---

## Purpose

The goal is to help traders:
- Understand market structure
- Recognize volatility shifts
- Develop disciplined decision-making
- Think in probabilities, not predictions

---

## Philosophy

Markets move due to participation and liquidity.
No indicator predicts the future — only context improves decisions.

This project values:
- Clarity over complexity  
- Process over outcomes  
- Consistency over emotion  

---

## Who This Is For

This content is for traders who:
- Want to learn how markets behave  
- Prefer structure over hype  
- Value long-term understanding  

---

## Important Notice

All content is for educational purposes only.
Nothing here should be considered financial advice.
